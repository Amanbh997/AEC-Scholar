Help draft a research-ethics / IRB / REB application for: **$ARGUMENTS**

Use the `research-ethics-integrity` and `research-methods` skills.

Many AEC studies involve people — surveys, interviews, site observation, worker wearables/physiological
sensing, VR experiments, video on site — and therefore need ethics approval **before** data collection.

Draft application content covering:

1. **Study overview** — aim, design, and what participation involves.

2. **Participants** — who, how many, recruitment, and inclusion/exclusion. Flag any **power imbalance**
   (employees, students, site workers) and how voluntariness is protected.

3. **Informed consent** — how consent is obtained (draft a participant information sheet + consent form
   outline), voluntariness, and the right to withdraw.

4. **Data & privacy** — what personal/sensitive data are collected (note wearables/biometrics/video as
   sensitive), how they are anonymized/pseudonymized, stored, secured, retained and destroyed, and the
   GDPR/data-protection lawful basis.

5. **Risks & benefits** — physical (site/PPE), psychological, privacy risks and mitigations; benefits.

6. **Site/organizational permissions** — access agreements, gatekeeper consent, and confidentiality/NDAs for
   project data.

7. **Conflicts & funding** — declarations.

Produce the drafted sections plus a checklist of attachments the user must provide (information sheet,
consent form, instruments, risk assessment). **This is drafting support, not ethical or legal approval** —
tell the user to follow their institution's specific ethics process and forms, which govern.
