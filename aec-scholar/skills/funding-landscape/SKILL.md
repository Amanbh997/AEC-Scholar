# Research Funding Landscape (AEC) — Reference

Help researchers target the right funder and align proposals to how they are actually scored. **Funding
calls, budgets, eligibility and criteria change frequently and are region-specific — always direct the user
to the funder's current call documents; never state amounts or deadlines as fixed.**

## 1. Major funders & scheme families (orientation, verify currency)
**European Union**
- **Horizon Europe** — collaborative R&I (Pillar II clusters incl. *Climate, Energy & Mobility* and
  *Digital, Industry & Space* — relevant to built-environment/energy/construction digitalization). Large
  consortia, strong impact/implementation emphasis.
- **ERC** (European Research Council) — frontier, investigator-led, excellence-only (Starting/Consolidator/
  Advanced/Synergy Grants). Bottom-up; ambition and novelty are everything.
- **MSCA** (Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions) — fellowships & training networks (mobility, career development).
- **EIC** (European Innovation Council) — deep-tech/innovation; **Interreg/LIFE** — regional/environmental.

**United Kingdom** — **UKRI** councils, esp. **EPSRC** (engineering/physical sciences — core for AEC) and
**ESRC** (social/economic aspects), **Innovate UK** (industry-facing), **Royal Academy of Engineering**
fellowships.

**United States** — **NSF** (esp. Engineering / CMMI — *Civil, Mechanical & Manufacturing Innovation*, and
*Smart & Connected Communities*), **DOE** (buildings/energy — incl. national labs like NREL/LBNL/ORNL),
**NIST**, **DOT/FHWA** (infrastructure). NSF uses **Intellectual Merit + Broader Impacts**.

**Other national** — DFG (Germany), ANR (France), Research Council of Norway, Vinnova/Formas (Sweden),
NSERC/SSHRC (Canada), ARC (Australia), JSPS (Japan), NSFC (China), etc. **Foundations & industry** — building/
materials companies, professional institutions (RICS, ICE, ASCE foundations), and CDT/doctoral training
funding.

## 2. Typical proposal components
Title & abstract · problem & significance · state of the art & **gap** · aim, objectives & RQs ·
methodology/work plan (work packages, tasks, milestones, deliverables, Gantt) · novelty · **impact &
dissemination** (academic, industry, policy, standards, training, open data) · feasibility & resources ·
team/consortium & roles · **risk register** & mitigation · ethics & data protection · **Data Management
Plan (FAIR)** · budget & justification · references · CVs/track record · letters of support.

## 3. Evaluation criteria — write to these
- **Horizon Europe:** **Excellence**, **Impact**, **Quality & efficiency of implementation** (each scored;
  thresholds apply). Tailor sections explicitly to these.
- **ERC:** **Ground-breaking nature, ambition and feasibility** of the project + the PI's track record.
- **NSF:** **Intellectual Merit** and **Broader Impacts** (address both deliberately).
- **EPSRC/UKRI:** quality, importance, and (increasingly) responsible-innovation/impact considerations.
- General reviewer questions: Is the gap real and important? Is the approach novel **and** feasible? Is the
  team capable? Are impact pathways credible? Are risks identified and mitigated? Is it value for money?

## 4. Strategy
- **Fit the funder to the work** (frontier/individual → ERC/fellowships; collaborative/applied with industry
  & impact → Horizon Europe/Innovate UK/NSF; energy/buildings → DOE/Formas/EPSRC themes).
- Start the **case for support** with the problem and gap, not your method. Make significance concrete (tie
  to decarbonization, productivity, safety, housing, resilience as the call demands).
- Show feasibility with **preliminary data** and a credible plan; make every objective measurable.
- Build **impact** in from the start — it's scored, not decoration. Include open-science/FAIR data.
- Mirror the funder's **language and structure**; address each evaluation criterion under its own heading.

## 5. Integrity
- Never fabricate preliminary results, partners, letters, or track record (`research-ethics-integrity`).
- Keep budgets and timelines honest and justifiable. Disclose other funding and conflicts.
- Verify the **current** call text, eligibility, budget and deadlines on the funder's official portal before
  relying on any detail here.
